This Privacy Policy explains how Space Ball Run (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the App.

1) Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal information. Space Ball Run does not require you to create an account, and it does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

2) Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store basic game data locally on your device (for example: settings or progress) using browser storage such as localStorage or cache. This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

3) Ads & Analytics

Space Ball Run does not include advertising SDKs and does not use analytics tools that track you across apps or websites.

4) Permissions

The App does not request sensitive permissions such as location, contacts, microphone, or camera.

5) Third-Party Services

The App is delivered through your device’s web engine (e.g., Chrome / Android System WebView). Those components may have their own privacy policies and may process technical data required to load web content. We do not control or receive that data.

6) Children’s Privacy

Space Ball Run does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect personal data at all, there is no user profile or tracking.

7) Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will change the “Last updated” date on this page.
